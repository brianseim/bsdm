# Shavrak Asha

**Race:** Half-Elf

**Player:** [[../Players/Sean|Sean]]
**Campaign:** [[../index|Mummy's Mask]]

---

## Backstory

Shavrak Asha was born to a Drow noble father and a human mother — a life defined from the start by divided loyalties and a world that didn't quite know what to make of him.

His father sent him to the Valashmai Jungle on a mission: recover an ancient artifact said to be the key to lifting a curse that has plagued the family for generations. The details of the curse were never fully explained to Shavrak — only that finding the artifact was essential, and that failure was not an option.

It was in the jungle that Shavrak first called upon his Eidolon, Streeaka. The summoning surprised even him — the bond felt different than he expected, the creature's presence warm and steady in a way that seemed almost at odds with the dark, tangled magic he'd grown up around. He and Streeaka made their way through the ruins together, but the mission ended in violence, and Shavrak awoke with fragments of memory he couldn't quite piece together.

Now he finds himself in Wati, drawn by the promise of ancient artifacts and the hope that somewhere in the Necropolis, he might find something that will break the curse and finally free his family — and himself — from whatever has bound them for so long.
