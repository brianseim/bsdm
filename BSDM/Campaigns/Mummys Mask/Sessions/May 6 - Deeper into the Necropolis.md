# May 6 — Deeper into the Necropolis

**Campaign:** [[../index|Mummy's Mask]]

---

*As told by a storyteller at the fireside of a midevel pub — one installment of a longer tale.*

---

Gather 'round, lads and lasses, and let me regale ye with a tale of adventure most perilous! The brave souls of the BSGamers ventured forth into the ancient and cursed House of Penthru, where dark forces stirred.

Leading the charge was Blade, a mighty warrior with a thirst for battle that could not be quenched. Alongside him stood Iverson, a cunning cleric whose spells could mend wounds and bolster his comrades. The stealthy Stryker crept through the shadows, ever watchful for danger. And Lishka, the cat-like ranger, lent her keen eyes and deadly aim to the group.

As they explored the crumbling estate, they encountered fell beasts — vicious, flying heads that could charm the unwary and drain their very vitality! The party fought bravely, but these foes were unlike any they had faced before. Blade's mighty blows glanced off their unearthly hides, while Iverson's healing magics struggled to keep up with the onslaught.

Just when all seemed lost, Xander the Zealot unleashed a torrent of arcane fury, blasting the foul creatures with searing acid! The tide of battle turned, and one by one, the party vanquished the demonic heads.

But their trials were far from over. As they delved deeper into the House of Penthru, they discovered a dark secret — a curse that had plagued the estate for centuries. Hair began to fall from their heads, and their senses dulled. The very air seemed to sap their strength.

Undaunted, the BSGamers pressed on, determined to uncover the truth and lift the ancient curse. With cunning and valor, they battled through hordes of serpents and hounds, ever closer to the heart of the mystery.

And so the tale continues, my friends. What further dangers and discoveries await our brave adventurers in the cursed House of Penthru? Only time will tell. But mark my words — the BSGamers are a hardy lot, and they shall not be easily deterred!
